Druid: Circle of Stone

Druids who join the circle of stone appreciate the abiotic components of nature as much as the living parts, and that the former outlasts the latter.


Circle Spells

Your connection to the earth grants you additional spells at 2nd, 3rd, 5th, 7th, and 9th level. Once you have access to these spells, you always have them prepared, they count as druid spells for you, and they don't count against the number of druid spells you can have prepared each day.

Druid Level Circle Spells
2rd Comprehend Languages, Earth Tremor
3rd Earthbind, Zone of Truth
5th Erupting Earth, Meld into Stone
7th Locate Creature, Stone Shape
9th Commune, Legend Lore

Stony Form

When you join the Circle of Stone at 2nd level, when you use your wild shape, you can instead enter a stony form. In this form, you gain the following benifets:
  -Your AC is equal to either 17, or 14 + your Dexterity modifier (your choice each time).
  -Your Strength equals 16, if it is not higher already.
  -You gain resitance to non-magical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.

Embedded Gems

Starting at 6th level, when you enter your stony form, powerful gems appear in it. Whenever you enter your stony form, roll 1d3, the number rolled equaling the number of gems that appear. For each gem, roll a d6, which determines the type of each gem, as according to the table below. Roll a d8 instead once you reach level 10 in this class, and a d10 when you reach level 14 in this class. While in your stony form, you may use an action to activate one of the gems, and the effect listed in the table takes affect. Once the gem is used, it disappears. Any gems that you haven't used when you exit the form do not carry over to the next time you enter it.

Gem Effect
1 Quartz One creature of your choice within 30 feet of you that you can sense regains 2d6 hit points
2 Amethyst One of the following effects of your choice ends on a creature of your choice within 30 feet of you that you can sense: Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed.
3 Garnet You regain 2d8 hit points.
4 Topaz Each creature within 30 feet of you that you are aware of either take 1d4 radiant damage, or regain 1d4 hit points, at your discretion.
5 Aquamarine One creature of your choice within 30 feet of you that you can sense does an extra 2d8 radiant damage against one other creature of your choice on the next attack it makes, provided it makes that attack on its next turn.
6 Peridot Each creature of your choice within 30 feet of you, including you, gains 1d6 + its own Wisdom modifier temporary hit points. All creatures use the same roll.
7 Sapphire Each creature of you choice within 30 feet of you have advantage on Wisdom saving throws until the end of your turn after your next turn.
8 Emerald Choose up to your Proficiency bonus of creatures within 30 feet of you that you can sense, including yourself. Each creature has advantage on the next attack roll, ability check, or saving throw it makes. If a creature fails the attack roll, ability check, or saving throw regardless, it gains a +1 bonus on the next one it makes.
9 Ruby Each creature of you choice that you can sense within 30 feet of you has advantage on attack rolls that use Strength and do an extra 2d4 force damage with their weapons and spells until the end of your turn after your next turn.
10 Diamond You gain true sight out to a range of 120 feet until the end of your fourth turn after this one.

Sturdy

When you reach 10th level, your ties to the earth magically enhance you. You gain advantage against saving throws against being knocked prone, and an additional +2 bonus if you are touching earth or stone with your bare skin. Also, if you are at your maximum hit points and damage from an attack would reduce you to 0 hit points, you are instead left with 1 Hit Point.

One with the Earth

At the 14th level, you gain the ability to manipulate earth with your thoughts and motions. You can cast the following spells without expending a spell slot and with only somatic components: Earth Tremor(1st level), Meld into Stone(2nd level), Erupting Earth(3rd level), Stone Shape(4th), Wall of Stone(5th), Bones of the Earth(6th), Move Earth(6th). When you cast one of these spells in this way, you must succeed on a Wisdom check, the DC of which equals 10 + the spells level, or the spell fails and the action is wasted. This feature also gives you tremorsense out to 60 feet.
